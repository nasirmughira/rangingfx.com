## Why launch speed matters

Trading audiences expect websites that feel instant. When they land on a fintech site, every millisecond matters for trust and retention.

- Fast navigation
- Clear value propositions
- Eye-catching data visuals

## Build for mobile-first investors

Design for small screens first, then scale up. Mobile-first architecture ensures the experience is optimized for modern traders on the go.

## Optimize for search

SEO isn't optional. Use structured content, a clear heading hierarchy, and a blog system that keeps your brand top of mind for relevant keywords.
